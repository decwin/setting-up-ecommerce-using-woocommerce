Setting Up E-commerce Using WooCommerce

In this lesson you will learn the basics of using the WooCommerce plugin for WordPress.

In this Presentation, you will learn:

  1. Installation.
  2. General store settings.
  3. Adding and editing products.
  4. order management.
  5. Sales reporting.
  6. The system status report.

Log into your WordPress Dashboard and go to Plugins > Add New. In the search field, type “WooCommerce” and click Search Plugins.

Enter your store's location, currency accepted and the types of products you plan to sell.

Setup the payment methods used by your shop. You must register with your chosen payment method before you can accept payments using their services.

Set your shipping options. You can include weight and dimension units used to ship your products.

Your store is now setup and ready to add your first product! Click Create a product button to add a product to your store.
